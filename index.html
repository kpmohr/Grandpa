POEM

Inside a Rainbow

I stepped inside a rainbow In a distant foreign land At the edge of heaven’s reflection In a place some call Ireland

There the spirit of my history took me gently in its hand

My feet were firmly planted on this strangely familiar ground Where my ancestors walked before me long before America was found

My heart and mind were filled with all the visions of the past As I wondered just how long this miracle would last

I felt a strength and happiness and all of life’s great joys As I gazed upon the radiant faces of all of history’s little girls and boys

Then suddenly I realized why my life has been such a joy I’ve been living in a rainbow since first I was a boy

I have know life’s greatest pleasure, that of family and of friends You see that’s the special prize at one of rainbows ends And then the joy of the universe cause my soul to sigh As I followed all of those brilliant colors as they reached into the sky

My mind then embraced the notion of what I could take home And it raced to capture all the visions and the verses that would soon become this poem

So come into my rainbow Ahh I’ve had it long enough I’ll teach you how to use it It will help to lift you up

Now look out through our rainbow Do it for a while The longer that you look The more it makes you smile

Over there! Do you see that special glow Ahh now that’s the part that helps you as you grow And there – over there! Do you see the extra gleam Now that’s the part that helps you when you dream There are seven colors in a rainbow But you are the eighth in mine You’re the extra color in my rainbow You’re the bit that makes it shine

So come in and stand beside me Here – close enough to hold my hand I know you’re going to like it I’ve been here since time began

And now I’ll share the rainbow’s special secret I’ll tell you where happiness can be found It’s at the other end of our rainbow Where ever it touches the ground
